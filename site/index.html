Asteroids in HTML5

Alas for your ancient coal-fired browser that doesn't support the canvas element. No game for you until you upgrade. Maybe try Firefox or Google Chrome.

Keyboard Controls:

  • Left Arrow: Rotate spaceship left.
  • Right Arrow: Rotate spaceship right.
  • Up Arrow: Spaceship thrusters.
  • Down Arrow: Shields.
  • Spacebar: Fire main pew-pew weapon.
  • Escape: Pause game.

Work-In-Progress for the UW's HTML5 Web and Mobile Game Development program, Winter 2013, taught by Thomas O'Connor.
